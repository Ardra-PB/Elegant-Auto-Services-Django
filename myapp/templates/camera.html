Your driving experience becomes safer and smoother when your car’s camera calibration and radar systems are perfectly aligned. These systems work silently in the background—guiding lane changes, braking, parking, reversing, and adaptive cruise control.

At Elegant Way Auto Maint, we use state-of-the-art calibration equipment to ensure your ADAS systems function exactly as designed by the manufacturer.

Why Camera & Radar Calibration Is Important

  • Ensures accurate lane assist & collision avoidance
  • Improves braking response & adaptive cruise control
  • Prevents false warnings & system errors
  • Essential after windshield replacement or accidents

Common Reasons Calibration & Radar Fail

  • Faulty or damaged camera unit
  • Loose or damaged wiring connections
  • Malfunctioning media / head unit
  • Camera lens obstruction or tampering
  • Improperly mounted radar sensors
