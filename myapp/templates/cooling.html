Cooling System Repair

Did you know that engine overheating is one of the most common problems on the roads of Sharjah? Overheating can severely damage your car’s engine and transmission. It is therefore imperative to keep the engine cooling system in good condition by servicing it from time to time.

Main Components of the Engine Cooling System

  • Thermostat
  • Upper hose
  • Radiator
  • Fan
  • Water pump
  • Transmission cooler
  • Lower hose
  • Reserve tank
  • Heater core
  • Heater hoses
  • Pressure cap

All these components are the heart and soul of the engine. If they do not function properly, the engine can be damaged seriously.

How Do You Know the Cooling System Needs Servicing?

  • When the car engine is overheating
  • The car stops automatically while driving
  • The radiator is emitting steam
  • Your car AC is not turning on or off
  • The warning light on the dashboard is on
  • The coolant is disappearing

Why Do Car Engines Overheat?

Engines overheat when excess heat cannot escape from the engine compartment. This can happen due to cooling system leakage, non-functioning fans, broken water pumps, or clogged coolant hoses.

How to Prevent the Engine from Overheating?

Regular inspection is the best way to keep your engine healthy. If any faulty parts or blockages are found, they must be fixed immediately. If the problem persists, it is best to take your vehicle to a professional service.
