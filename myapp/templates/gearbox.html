Expert Gearbox Repair & Transmission Service in Sharjah

Smooth shifting, optimal torque & long-lasting performance

The gearbox is the second most essential component of your car. It transfers power from the engine to the wheels, allowing the vehicle to achieve the required speed and torque efficiently. It also enables reverse movement, ensuring complete driving control.

For a seamless and safe driving experience, maintaining your car’s gearbox in excellent condition is crucial. At Elegant Way Auto Maint, we provide professional gearbox and transmission repair services using advanced diagnostic equipment.

Signs Your Car Needs Gearbox Repair

  • Delayed or no gear response
  • Gear slipping during acceleration
  • Unusual noises from the gearbox
  • Burning smell from the transmission
  • Fluid leakage under the vehicle
  • Check engine or transmission warning light

Common Gearbox & Transmission Problems

  • Transmission fluid leakage: Caused by damaged seals or fluid pans, leading to overheating and internal damage.
  • Worn clutch plates: Results in grinding sensations and poor gear engagement.
  • Damaged shift fork: Prevents gears from locking correctly and causes dangerous gear slipping.
  • Low fluid levels: Increases heat and friction, severely damaging gearbox components.

Our Gearbox Repair Process

Our technicians begin with a comprehensive test drive to identify gearbox behavior. We then perform advanced diagnostics to evaluate control units, transmission oil condition, and internal faults. Depending on the findings, the gearbox is serviced, repaired, refurbished, or replaced.

After completion, a final diagnostic test ensures flawless performance and smooth gear transitions.
