ABOUT THE COMPANY

"Elegant Way Auto Maint Services is a complete solution for all automotive repair and service needs in the UAE."

Founded in 2019, Elegant Way Auto Maint Services was created with the goal of delivering a higher standard of vehicle repair and maintenance than traditional car dealerships—while offering services at more competitive and transparent prices.

Today, we operate branches exclusively in Sharjah, ensuring convenient and reliable vehicle servicing for our customers. We are actively planning further expansion across the GCC.

We offer the same range of repairs and services as authorized luxury car dealerships, but with added advantages such as affordable pricing, flexible scheduling, and a customer-focused approach.

With more than 18 years of combined dealership experience, our expert team handles everything from routine maintenance to complex engine repairs.

As a state-of-the-art, independently owned facility, we deliver exceptional expertise at significantly lower costs—sometimes up to 50% less—without compromising quality.

We have also launched a mobile application for easy service booking.

Our excellence is recognized with 5-Star ESMA Certification and ISO Certification.
