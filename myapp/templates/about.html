ABOUT THE COMPANY


"Elegant Way Auto Maint Services is a complete solution for all automotive repair and service needs in the UAE."


Founded in 2019, Elegant Way Auto Maint Services was created with the goal of delivering a higher standard of vehicle repair and maintenance than traditional car dealerships—while offering services at more competitive and transparent prices.


Today, we operate branches exclusively in Sharjah, ensuring convenient and reliable vehicle servicing for our customers. To make vehicle servicing even more convenient for our customers across the GCC, we are actively planning further expansion.


We offer the same range of repairs and services as authorized luxury car dealerships, but with added advantages such as affordable pricing, flexible scheduling, and a customer-focused approach. We are not just an alternative—we are the preferred service center for luxury car repair and maintenance in the UAE.


With more than 18 years of combined dealership experience, our expert team is equipped to handle everything from routine maintenance to complex engine repairs. Whether your vehicle is new or old, you can rely on us for high-quality workmanship without the excessive costs typically associated with dealerships.


As a state-of-the-art, independently owned and operated auto repair facility, our workshop functions differently from dealer service centers. This allows us to deliver exceptional expertise and craftsmanship at significantly lower costs—sometimes up to 50% less—without compromising on quality.


To further enhance customer convenience, we have recently launched a mobile application that allows you to schedule vehicle services easily from anywhere.


Our commitment to excellence has been recognized with 5-Star ESMA Certification and ISO Certification, reflecting the high standards and quality of service we consistently deliver.
